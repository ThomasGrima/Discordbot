[RULES]

General Rules
Please refer to discord rules as these also reflect in game.
Loopholing any rules is prohibited.
Use common sense while playing, and contact staff if you’re unsure.

Note: All rule violations are handled at staff discretion and follow the regular strike system. Strikes, mutes, and bans may be issued depending on severity.

G1 – General Conduct
- No NSFW topics or inappropriate comments.
- Keep it friendly; GG is fine, but avoid GGEZ or disrespectful comments.

G2 – Exploits & Bugs
- Do not abuse in-game bugs, glitches, or file manipulation.
Examples include (but are not limited to):
  - Triple Bite
  - Double Jump
  - Mosa chain grabbing
  - Archerfish shooting through solid objects
  - Flier dart bug during engagements
  - Trinket duplication/sharing
  - Gore glitching
  - Purposely meshing your body (tail is fine; unavoidable meshing is okay). No meshing during combat.
  - Modified skin files to gain bio
Staff may ban anyone suspected of bio hacking.

G3 – Self Feeding
- Do not eat your own or your group’s gore.
- Do not break and eat your own/group eggs.
- Do not purposely hand over gore or force-give gore to another player.

G4 – Spawn Protection
- Do not attack players for 1 minute after they spawn or after you spawn.
- Safe-logging during engagements is not allowed.

G5 – Server Restarts
- 5-minute protection grace period applies before and after each restart.
- Track uptime via Esc → Show Details.
- Engagements already in progress before the grace period may continue.
- Server crashes trigger a new 5-minute grace period.

G6 – Location Information
- Do not drop or share player locations outside your group.
- Sharing locations is considered illegal mixpacking.

G7 – Group Conduct & Scouting / Mixpacking
- Do not scout by receiving information on one dino and giving it to others outside your group.
- Do not use one dino to scout/duel and then swap to another to attack the group you located.
- Keep a safe distance when near other groups if not grouped.
- Do not attack group members without consent.
- Do not assist, feed, or protect players outside your group.

G8 – Post Group Conduct / After Leaving a Group
After leaving a group:
- Wait 1 minute before hunting former group members.
- You may not take their PG with you; PG stays with the group.
- You may not leave a group, kill a player, and return to your previous group immediately.
- Changing groups inherits all previous timers.
Example: Kill with group A → join group B → group B may not attack the same target until timer expires.

G9 – Revenge & Targeting
- Revenge killing or intentionally targeting players is prohibited.
- Includes declaring you will target or returning early to hunt someone.
- You may not hunt the same group more than 3 times in 2 hours.

G10 – Impersonation
- Do not impersonate staff or other players.
- Impersonation to gain favor or exploit trust is strictly prohibited.

G11 – Duels
- Duels are at your own risk.
- Duels do not count as engagements and do not protect you from other players.
- Do not use duels to exploit engagements.
