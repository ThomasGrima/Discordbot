[RULES]

[STAFF] Enforcement: Staff may issue warnings, strikes, mutes, or bans depending on severity. Staff discretion applies to all cases.
[COMMUNITY] Core Principle: Maintain respect, fairness, and enjoyment. The Great Valley exists for friendly play and mutual fun.

[Toxicity Ruleset]
[T1] Toxic Behaviour: No harassment, insults, bullying, gloating, or antagonizing. Avoid sarcasm meant to mock. Respect others in chat and DMs.
[T2] Passive-Aggressive Remarks: Do not dismiss others with phrases like “cry more”, “skill issue”, or “snowflake”. “It’s just a joke” is not an excuse.
[T3] Rudeness: Sarcasm, dismissive comments, mocking typos, or eye-roll replies like “K” are toxic.
[T4] Targeting / Harassment: Do not repeatedly follow or attack a player after they ask to stop. Repeated kills, spawn camping, or taunting are harassment.
[T5] Escalation: Do not retaliate or prolong arguments. Walk away or report instead.
[T6] Public Shaming: No mocking kill messages, “GGEZ”, targeted screenshots, or memes. Win with grace.
[T7] Respect Staff: Do not argue staff decisions publicly or make passive-aggressive remarks about rules. Discuss concerns privately.
[T8] Anti-Racism / Xenophobia: No racial slurs, stereotypes, or jokes about nationality, accent, or culture.

[MACROING] Definition: Using scripts, software, or hardware to automate gameplay actions (auto-feed, auto-attack, AFK macros) is forbidden.
[MACROING] Allowed Use: Only macros for non-gameplay tasks that do not affect survival, combat, or progression are allowed (e.g., accepting eggs briefly).
[MACROING] Strike System: 1-Warning, 2-Kick, 3-1h Ban, 4-5h Ban, 5-12h Ban, 6-24h Ban, 7+-Long-term/Permanent Ban at staff discretion.

[GENERAL] Loopholing: Exploiting unclear wording or technicalities in rules is prohibited. Use common sense; when unsure, ask staff.
[G1] General Conduct: No NSFW topics or inappropriate comments. GG is fine, GGEZ or disrespect is not.
[G2] Exploits & Bugs: Do not abuse bugs, glitches, or file edits (triple bite, double jump, meshing, trinket duping, mosa chain grabbing, flier dart bug during engagements, gore glitching etc.). Modified skins for bio gain = ban.
[G3] Self-Feeding: Do not eat your own or group gore/eggs. Do not force to give another player gore. Do not purposefully hand over a over a gore.
[G4] Spawn Protection: No attacking for 1 minute after spawn (you or others). No safe-logging mid-fight.
[G5] Restarts: 5-minute grace before/after server restarts. Engagements already active may continue. Server crashes also start the grace period.
[G6] Location Info: Do not share or drop player locations outside your group. Sharing = mixpacking.
[G7] Group Conduct: No cross-group scouting, swapping dinos to exploit info, or assisting outsiders. Keep distance from other groups. No team-killing. Do not attack group members without consent.
[G8] Post-Group Conduct: After leaving a group, wait 1 minute before hunting ex-members. PG stays with original group. Changing groups keeps timers.
[G9] Revenge / Targeting: No revenge hunts or repeated targeting. Do not hunt same group >3 times in 2 hours. Includes declaring you will target or return early to hunt someone.
[G10] Impersonation: Do not impersonate staff or players. Gaining trust under false identity is bannable.
[G11] Duels: Duels are at your own risk and do not protect you from others. Do not exploit duel mechanics.
[G12] Shrine & Portal Camping: Camping is defined as hanging around the immediate area for an extended amount of time and waiting for players to bless and leave the safety of the shrine radius to then attack them. No camping portals or shrine radius. You may not initiate a hunt on players in the vicinity of the Shrine Radius (No growth debuff). Do not intentionally run into shrine radius to body-deny players.
[G13] Egg/Nest Conduct: Do not intentionally break eggs. Accidents happen; unattended/Abandoned nests are free game. Abandoned nests count as nests without any hatchlings or adults nearby. Do not sit on nests to avoid being hunted. If a parent asks you to leave their egg please do so failing to do this may result in staff kicking you from the server.
[G14] Egg Limits: General limit: 25 eggs per mating pair. Minor excess is fine, but 30+ eggs may result in a strike. Monitor eggs before resets to avoid violations.
[G15] 2-Call Rule: After 2-calling someone outside your group, wait 2 minutes before attacking. Does not prevent them from attacking you if they don’t 2-call back.
[G16] Legging Gores: Do not leg any gore (PG, Fish, Spawn). Legging causes lag; 3 offenses = additional strike. Sniff the gore to make sure you can carry it first and look for the weight icons on the side.
[G17] Intimidation: Do not intimidate others outside legal engagements. Maintain appropriate distance near nests or areas claimed by others. Please utilize local chat to inform someone of their intim and wait a minute for a response. If they openly refuse to move, or are afk and you were there first, you are allowed to attack them regardless of player gore on either side. If they were there first, you may not intim them out. Body down rules still apply!
[G18] Growth & GP Accuracy: Do not exaggerate growth or GP when advertising eggs. Rounding to 0.1 is allowed.
[G19] Resource Theft: Do not attack to steal trinkets, idols, resources, or gore (please follow creature specific contest/stealing rules in regards to gore theft). Do not take resources from another players mouth outside of your group. Ground items: trinkets/resources are free to take. Gore may only be stolen under proper conditions (scavenger rules, aquatic vs terrestrial, etc.).
[G20] Reports: Third-party reports for banter in global/local chat are not allowed unless directed at you.
[G21] Blocking Paths: Do not purposely block high-traffic areas (caves, bridges, ramps) while refusing to let players past. Players must 2-call before passing through. Not 2-calling allows others to defend themselves. Creatures carrying claimed gore are exempt from needing to 2 call as they cannot attack while carrying/owning gore. Herbivores follow H1; carnivores follow C3 if in possession of gore.
[G22] Spam Calling:  Repeated use of call keys (1/2/3/Z call) without valid gameplay reason is prohibited. Especially when used to: Annoy players, Disrupt events, Ignore requests from staff or other players.

[Engagement Rules]
[E1] Engagement Start: Begins when damage, purposeful Z-call, grab, spray, egg or baby theft, or gore theft occurs. Must deal damage every 5m or engagement ends. No switching targets until timer ends. A contest is initiated.
[E1a]: If an engagement starts while you’re not present, you have 2 minutes to reach your group.
[E1b]: Damage must occur at least every 5 minutes to keep the engagement active. No damage for 5 minutes automatically ends the engagement.
[E1c]: You may not switch targets until the 5-minute no-damage timer ends.
[E2] Timers / 1 Body Down: 1 body down = 20m timer; no body = 5m timer. Exceptions: small dinos (Velo, Ory, PT, AI) in mixed groups don’t count as body. See CO3 for recontest timer.
[E3] Juveniles / Scentless: Under 0.8 growth or no scent don’t count as body unless targeted first. Attacking hatchlings starts an engagement. Abuse is prohibited (e.g., targeting adults first, then turning on hatchlings mid-fight).
[E3a]: If hatchlings and/or juveniles are targeted within the same area this will be treated as an engagement by the attacking player. Once a body drops it will begin a 20 minute timer between the attacker and hatchling / juveniles within that area
[E4] Simultaneous Deaths: If two bodies drop at the same time: opposing sides may exchange gore or contest; winner takes all. Solo vs solo deaths: first to return to the gore claims all gore and cannot be recontested by the party they just fought. Solo vs group deaths: group claims both their party's gore and the solo player gore. A 20-minute timer starts between both parties involved.
[E5] Interference: Do not interfere in another group’s engagement. Joining a group mid-engagement to assist is prohibited. E1a rule for reference. Aquatic-specific exceptions are listed in the Aquatic Rules section.
[E6] Ending Engagements: Ends when 1 body drops, All attacking members sit/lay (non-solo group), A solo attacking player lays down, No damage dealt for 5 minutes. The defending party may not sit/lay to signal an end. Solo aquatics are exempt from resting rules. You may not return to participate in an engagement if you were killed. Returning to watch is fine but you may not participate or help in any way.
[E7] Witnessing Engagements: Witnessing = physically seeing the engagement (hearing does not count). You may not attack or contest  participating parties for 5 minutes after the fight ends. They may still attack you if you enter combat range or lack fresh player gore.
[E8] Portal Restrictions: Do not fight inside portal areas (mushroom ring). May not remain in a portal area for more than 2 minutes. Engaging within 20 seconds of using a portal is prohibited. Packmates may use portals to reach an engagement, but not to escape one.
[E9] Respawning, Longing, and Prolonging Engagements: Do not force respawn or safe-log once an engagement involving you has started. Engagements should not be prolonged for trolling or toxic means. (I.E. As a mosa do not repeatedly grab someone and drag them out further into the ocean without attacking or attempting to drown.) Note: Yes some engagements may take a long time but if you have the ability to end it quicker then use that method.
[E10] Gore Forfeiting: If attacked by a party and you win, you may choose to either keep or give up the gore. This applies only to the defending party. If the attacking party wins they must take the gore. This also applies to if you are contested for your own gore. (You must still keep your original gore)
[E11] Gore Rights: Meat eaters in a herd may not eat from/claim a gore that is dropped after an engagement ends if the opposing party has 2 or more players. They may only eat from the dropped gore if the opposing party is a solo player. If a carni group loses to a herbivore group they MUST take the gore of their fallen packmate

[Contest Rules]
[CO1] Contesting Gore: To contest a gore: One group member must be able to reach AND move the gore when it is not on flat ground. (I.E. Up on a rock or ledge). You must 3-call to the defending party. Give 20 seconds for them to respond. Contesting is for all gores; must claim all if you win. All contesting players must be in LOS and immediate area. Must give space; do not stand on top of the defenders. Defending party accepts by attacking or 3-calling back. No interaction with gore during contest. If no response within 20 seconds → contest is automatically accepted.
[CO1a] Small Tiers: Velos/Changs/PT/Trope/AI in mid/apex packs cannot start contests. AI cannot contest at all.
[CO1b] Forfeiture: If defenders accept but fail to fight or deal damage in 1m, gore is forfeited.
[CO2] Ending Contest: Ends when defenders forfeit (2,4,5-call or local chat), one player dies, or both die simultaneously (defenders keep own + contesting gore; contesters take killed player’s gore).
[CO3] Contesting After Engagement: Losing side has 10s to contest fallen member’s gore if it wasn’t already a contest. Aquatics and terrestrials cannot contest across types.
[CO4] Post-Contest Rules: Losing party must leave the area. Contesting party must allow the losing party to move eggs/resources. No re-contesting until timer expires. Inside natural world events losing party does not need to leave their spot

[Carnivore Rules]
[C1] Gore Ownership: Players with fresh player gore cannot start engagements until it’s contested. Gore is claimed by Body drop from an engagement, Interacting with it (eating, carrying, or sitting next to it). Clear ownership: Must be close enough to the gore to bite, push, or block it from others - (Directly next to it or within 1 dino length horizontally of the dino they're playing away from it.). The following does not offer protection:  spawn gore, legs, fish gore, eggs, baby gores (under 0.8s - unseeable by non nest raiders).
[C1a] Gore Handling: Always sniff before picking up; do not intentionally leg gore.
[C2] Abandonment: Leaving gore, switching dinos, or respawning without return = abandonment.
[C2a] Temporary Absence: Allowed for water, blessing in a group (1 must stay with gore), weather forces you to leave, or gore spoils. If attacked away from gore you may defend yourself, you cannot return to it for protection and packmates may not leave gore to assist. At least one group member must remain with the gore at all times. Players may not hunt, scout, or start engagements while away from their gore
[C2b] Event Restrictions: Do not remove gore to despawn it intentionally.
[C3] Interaction with other partiy's gore: Players may not interact with another group’s/players claimed gore. Doing so gives that group/player the right to attack without warning. Entering the hit space of a group/player with gore may result in being attacked without warning.
Exception: Entering the hit space of a player physically on gore is not a violation.
[C4] Cooldown After Gore: After finishing or losing all gores, wait 1 minute before hunting again. This includes your gore despawning via world event ending.
Excluded: Yellow/Green regurgitated Palaeophis gores
Using this mechanic to bypass timers is considered a loophole.
[C5] Carnivore Gore: Carnivores must 2-call and announce in local chat before taking gore from another herd.

(Herbivores cannot intentionally walk through Carnivores to start an engagement.) If you attack someone that enters your hit space you may continue to attack them until they 2 call and haven't already attacked you back. If they 2 call you must stop attacking. Do not chase someone who has entered your hit space after you have begun attacking. If they move out of your space, leave them alone.
[H2] Herbivores may 3-call after an engagement ends to continue the fight. Carnivores must 3-call back to accept. Max two engagements; after second, normal timers apply.
[H3] Water Claims: Herds must have 3 HERBIVORE adults present in order to claim a water source. Herds must state in local that they are claiming the water source before attacking. Herds may not push or intimidate players that were in the immediate area out of the area whilst claiming a water source. A herd can only claim ONE water source at a time. (Leaving to get food is fine as long as you have 3 adults still near the water source) If at least 3 dinos are not visible at the claimed water source, you lose your claim to the water for 30 minutes. If all herd members agree, the herd may state their location in chat. A herd may 3 call aquatics out of a claimed water source if it does not connect to the ocean.

The contesting party must allow the losing party to gather their eggs/children.

You may not try to kill a player on gore then attempt to steal after failing to kill the player.) If you choose to steal you may defend yourself AFTER taking damage.
